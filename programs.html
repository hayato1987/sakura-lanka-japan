Specialized Employment Programs in Japan

Professional Skill

Specified Skilled Worker (SSW)

Designed for individuals with specific expertise and Japanese language proficiency. High salaries and long-term residency options.

👵

① Caregiving

Physical assistance and daily life support in elderly care facilities or home care. High demand with long-term needs; Japanese conversation skills are essential.

🧹

② Building Cleaning

Cleaning services for hotels, commercial facilities, and offices. Easy to enter for beginners with stable demand; focused on quality management and training.

⚙️

③ Manufacturing (Machine & Electronics)

Covers casting, machining, and assembly processes. High skill level required with strong demand in regional factories; offers relatively high wages.

🏗️

④ Construction

Civil engineering and building site work. Continuous infrastructure demand ensures chronic labor shortages; physical contribution is valued.

🚢

⑤ Shipbuilding & Ship Machinery

Specialized field involving ship construction, welding, painting, and piping. Demand is concentrated in specific regions with very high retention rates.

🔧

⑥ Automobile Maintenance

Inspection, maintenance, and repair of vehicles. Highly specialized work requiring certification; chronic shortages in regional repair shops.

✈️

⑦ Aviation

Ground handling and aircraft maintenance in airport environments. High-value field with professional growth potential and specialized training.

🏨

⑧ Accommodation

Hospitality, front desk, and cleaning in hotels and ryokans. Rapidly expanding due to tourism recovery; Japanese and hospitality skills are key.

🌾

⑨ Agriculture

Vegetable/fruit cultivation and livestock management. Seasonal work centered in regional areas; usually includes housing provision for a secure start.

🐟

⑩ Fishery

Operation on fishing vessels or aquaculture work. Demanding environment but critical for regional industries; long-term employment is the standard.

🍱

⑪ Food & Beverage Manufacturing

Processing and production lines in food factories. Hygiene management is paramount; stable demand allows for large-scale recruitment.

🍽️

⑫ Food Service

Customer service and cooking assistance in restaurants. Chronic shortages in urban centers; requires Japanese proficiency and hospitality mindset.

🚚

⑬ Automobile Transport

Truck, bus, and taxi driving. Demand has surged due to logistics challenges; driver's license and safety training are crucial.

🚃

⑭ Railway

Vehicle maintenance, track maintenance, and station operations. A newly established field prioritizing safety with high education standards.

🌲

⑮ Forestry

Management of forests and logging operations. Directly linked to regional revitalization; specialized work with persistent labor shortages.

🪵

⑯ Wood Industry

Processing, sawing, and drying of lumber. Stable field linked to construction demand; enables long-term employment through skill mastery.
